WHAT TO DO WHEN INTRODUCING NEW PLATFORMS:
==========================================

  * Platforms.getPlatformList()

  * review CMakeLists.txt

  * update packageVar.cmake

  * extend BST_DefaultSettings.cmake

  * ToolBOS.conf: compile host assignment for Zen Build Mode, or None

  * list in documentation ("supported platforms", "compile hosts")

  * mention in ChangeLog
